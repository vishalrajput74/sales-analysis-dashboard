# Business Insights Report

## Key Findings

- West region generates highest revenue, showing strong market demand.
- South region has lowest sales and needs better marketing strategy.
- Technology category is most profitable.
- Sales peak in November and December due to seasonal demand.
- Top products contribute significant revenue and should be promoted.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing on high-performing regions.
- Improve strategy in low-performing regions.
- Promote top-selling products.
- Increase inventory during peak months.
